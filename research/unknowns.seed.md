# Unknowns Seed — 3D Drift Racer

## Seed Questions for Research

1. **Cannon.js vs Rapier for car physics** — Which gives better drift feel with less tuning effort?
2. **Three.js post-processing stack** — How to achieve neon glow without heavy performance cost?
3. **Low-poly car mesh creation** — Procedural generation vs simple primitives vs imported GLTF?
4. **Track design** — Closed circuit vs open world? Procedural vs handcrafted?
5. **Drift detection algorithm** — Slip angle threshold, how to smooth input for responsive yet stable drift?
6. **Camera shake/offset on drift** — What camera behaviors create the best "drift feel"?
7. **Boost mechanic implementation** — How is boost visually communicated and how does it affect physics?
8. **Score multiplier curve** — What drift angle/duration curve feels rewarding?
9. **HUD design** — What information does the player need and where on screen?
10. **Performance on mobile** — Can 60fps be sustained on mid-range mobile with Three.js + physics?
