## Source Brief
> A 3D drifting car racing game. Player controls a car on a track and scores points by executing controlled drifts. Game features: 3D graphics using Three.js, physics-based car handling with realistic drift mechanics, a racetrack environment, score system based on drift angle and duration, speed boost rewards for long drifts. Visual style: stylized/low-poly with vibrant colors, neon accents, and dynamic camera movement. Experience tier: premium. Grade: Personal Use. Project ID: 3d-drift-racer.
